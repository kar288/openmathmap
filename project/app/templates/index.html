The MathMap is a project being presently worked on at Jacobs University.

This project wants to develop and deploy novel interfaces that empower interested parties to find their way. We have developed a prototype map generation service based on MSC classifications and deployed the maps resulting from ZBMath data in OpenStreetMap.
